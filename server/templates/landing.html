How It Works

1

Sign in with your email

Click "Send Magic Link" in the extension or dashboard. No password needed — just check your email and click the link.

2

Study something and track it

Navigate to any learning resource, click the extension, and hit Start Timer. When you're done, stop the timer, rate your recall, and save.

3

Get smart revision reminders

The SM-2 algorithm calculates when you'll start forgetting. Problems you find easy appear less often. Tough ones come back sooner.

4

Revise and level up

Check your "Due for Revision" list daily. Rate your recall each time. Over time, every pattern becomes second nature.

Install the Extension

Get the browser extension to track problems with one click.

🌍 Chrome

  1. Download the extension: Download extension.zip
  2. Unzip the downloaded file
  3. Open chrome://extensions in Chrome
  4. Enable Developer mode (top right toggle)
  5. Click Load unpacked and select the unzipped folder
  6. Pin the extension from the puzzle icon in the toolbar

🧭 Safari

Safari extension is available on request. It requires a macOS/iOS native app wrapper built with Xcode.
